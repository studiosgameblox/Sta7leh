About Sta7leh

The digital souk built for your street, your town, your life.

Our Story

It all started in a small Lebanese town. One of our founders needed a phone charger urgently, but had no idea where to get it quickly. The shops had it — but they were invisible online. That’s when Sta7leh was born.

A marketplace designed for *you* and *your neighborhood*, combining tradition with technology. Sta7leh brings the spirit of local souks into your pocket.

Our Mission

We believe everyone should have access to local products, job opportunities, and trust-driven shopping. Our mission is to build a digital ecosystem that empowers local businesses, freelancers, and delivery agents — especially the youth.

Sta7leh is not just a marketplace. It's a movement toward human-first commerce.

Ultra-Connected Marketplace

Sta7leh is your digital souk — connecting buyers, sellers, and couriers in real time. Whether you're shopping, selling, or delivering, everything happens smoothly in one smart platform.
